# Opportunity qualification

Use this checklist for every lead.

## Required fields

### Demand scenario

- What business problem is visible?
- Is it notification, interaction, service, voice, AI workflow, or AI infrastructure?

### Best-fit offering

Choose one lead offering first:

- SMS
- 5G消息
- 95短号码语音
- 企业 AI Agent 应用
- LLM 聚合 / Routing

Then list cross-sell opportunities.

### Buyer / owner

Try to identify:

- owning company
- department
- procurement platform
- project owner or event organizer
- branch level: group / province / city / district
- province / city / district name when the buyer is an operator branch

### Commercial signal

Rank:

- high: tender / procurement / budget / pilot / explicit demand
- medium: ecosystem cooperation / public partner recruitment / solution collection
- low: indirect trend signal only

Operator-specific boost:

- province-level centralized tender with clear communication or AI scope = usually high
- repeated city-level procurement under one province = medium to high depending on rollout pattern
- district / county-level micro projects = medium unless replicable

### Timing

- immediate
- this quarter
- medium term
- watchlist

### Competitive risk

Check if this is likely dominated by:

- operator direct channel
- incumbent CPaaS / messaging vendor
- call center vendor
- cloud / AI platform vendor

Also ask:

- is there a local SI or channel partner requirement
- is this easier as a subcontract / ecosystem entry than a prime bid
- is qualification likely limited by operator vendor pool or prior framework

## Suggested next action format

Use one of:

- sales should contact
- partner BD should approach
- monitor next tender stage
- build tailored solution deck
- validate buyer and budget first
- map the province-city branch chain first
- identify local delivery / SI partner before outreach

## Scoring hint

You can use a simple 5-point fit score:

- 5 = direct, urgent, budget-linked
- 4 = clear scenario, likely actionable
- 3 = interesting but missing owner or timing
- 2 = weak signal
- 1 = not actionable now
