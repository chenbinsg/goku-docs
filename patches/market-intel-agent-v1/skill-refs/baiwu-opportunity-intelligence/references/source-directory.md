# Source directory

This file lists preferred source categories for recurring commercial scans.

## Source quality ladder

Prefer sources in this order:

1. official tender / procurement announcement page
2. official bid-winning / result page
3. official enterprise procurement or supplier portal page
4. official company news / ecosystem recruitment page
5. public bidding aggregation page that links to an official source
6. search-engine results page only as a discovery aid, never as final evidence

## Invalid final evidence

Do not cite these as final evidence:

- Baidu / Bing / Google search results pages
- internal workspace screenshot paths
- generic portal landing pages with no project detail
- unverified reposts that do not preserve the source chain

## Telecom source categories

- operator group procurement portals
- provincial branch procurement / supplier pages
- city branch announcement pages
- operator-affiliated cloud / digital subsidiary announcement pages
- public bidding result pages that reference operator branches

Representative platforms:

- 中国移动集中采购 / 供应链相关入口
- 中国电信采购与招标入口
- 中国联通采购招标入口
- 各省分公司公告或供应商页面
- 中国招标投标公共服务平台、千里马、政府采购云、采招网等聚合入口用于发现地市级公告

## Bank source categories

- bank head-office procurement pages
- branch / sub-branch announcement pages
- credit-card center / remote-banking center / service-center pages
- bank-affiliated technology or consumer-finance procurement pages
- public procurement pages referencing the bank entity

Representative patterns:

- 银行官网中的采购公告 / 招标公告栏目
- 信用卡中心、远程银行中心、客服中心的专项公告页
- 银行系消费金融公司的采购或技术升级公告页
- 六大行、主要股份行、重点城商行均应纳入日常扫描池，不只扫描一两家银行

## Fintech and collections source categories

- company announcement / procurement pages
- partner recruitment / supplier enrollment pages
- public bid pages
- company tech upgrade or customer-service transformation pages
- regulator-exposed or licensed-entity lists for buyer validation
- 新闻/RSS 线索可用于发现消金、催收、客服升级和 AI 客户运营早期信号，但进入高价值机会前仍需下钻到项目或官方来源

Representative patterns:

- 消费金融公司官网公告或采购栏目
- 催收 / 客服外包企业的供应商招募与系统升级公告
- 持牌机构名录，用于校验买方主体真实性

## Minimum evidence expectation

For a report with concrete leads, aim to include:

- at least 2 direct-source links from official or near-official pages
- at least 1 lead from outside a pure search-engine result page
- evidence text that points to a project, procurement stage, or buyer action

## Sparse-day rule

If only discovery pages are found and no direct-source pages are reachable,
say so explicitly in `本次搜索覆盖说明` and treat the result as low confidence.
