# Baiwu product map

This file is the base context for deciding whether an opportunity is commercially relevant to 百悟科技.

## Core offerings

### 1. SMS

Typical scenarios:

- verification code
- transaction notification
- service alert
- appointment and logistics reminder
- membership reach and reactivation

Strong signals:

- need for guaranteed reach
- legacy app users
- finance, retail, local services, logistics, education
- high-volume outbound notification

Key buyer roles:

- product owner
- operations
- customer service
- marketing CRM
- IT / platform team

### 2. 5G消息

Typical scenarios:

- rich media customer interaction
- service guide, menu, card, quick reply
- acquisition and conversion messaging
- enterprise service entry in native messaging

Strong signals:

- brand wants richer interaction than SMS
- operator ecosystem / 5G message mention
- interactive customer workflow
- campaign conversion and service automation

Key buyer roles:

- digital marketing
- customer growth
- product / digital channel
- enterprise innovation team

### 3. 95 short-code voice

Typical scenarios:

- hotline / customer service voice access
- outbound voice notice
- callback / service recovery
- authentication / confirmation voice

Strong signals:

- voice touchpoint required
- phone-first service user group
- call center transformation
- strong compliance or trust need

Key buyer roles:

- call center
- service operations
- customer support
- operations compliance

### 4. AIOS-based enterprise AI Agent applications

Typical scenarios:

- AI customer service agent
- AI sales assistant
- AI internal operations copilot
- knowledge-driven support workflow
- multi-step enterprise process orchestration

Strong signals:

- customer mentions AI assistant, copilot, agent, workflow automation
- service desk / operations workload is high
- knowledge base or SOP is already present
- need human + AI collaboration, audit, workflow control

Key buyer roles:

- CIO / CTO office
- digital transformation team
- customer service leader
- operations leader
- business systems owner

### 5. LLM aggregation and routing infrastructure

Typical scenarios:

- multi-model access
- cost optimization across models
- reliability / fallback routing
- vendor abstraction
- internal AI platform

Strong signals:

- customer is already deploying LLMs
- concern on cost, latency, availability, governance
- need unified AI gateway / routing / provider management

Key buyer roles:

- AI platform team
- infrastructure architect
- CTO office
- security / governance team

## Cross-sell logic

When you see:

- customer reach demand
  Then test: SMS + 5G消息 + 95语音

- service center modernization
  Then test: 95语音 + AI Agent + knowledge workflow

- AI transformation project
  Then test: AI Agent app + LLM routing + messaging touchpoint

- digital campaign / user operation
  Then test: SMS + 5G消息 + AI-driven engagement orchestration

## High-fit opportunity patterns

- government or SOE service-notification transformation
- banking / fintech / insurance customer contact modernization
- retail / ecommerce member reach and customer operations
- logistics and service reminder systems
- enterprise call center AI upgrade
- operator ecosystem cooperation
- enterprise internal AI platform rollout

## Low-fit patterns

- pure hardware procurement
- generic AI branding project with no business process owner
- projects requiring custom telecom capability outside Baiwu’s scope
- opportunities with no clear communication, service, or AI workflow angle
