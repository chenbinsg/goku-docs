# Fintech, consumer finance, collections, and customer-service map

This file helps the agent identify opportunities from fintech companies and
customer-contact-heavy financial service operators.

## Priority company types

- 消费金融公司
- 网络小贷 / 小额贷款公司
- 互联网金融平台
- 助贷 / 导流 / 风控服务商
- 催收服务商
- 智能客服服务商
- 金融客户运营平台

## Why this matters for Baiwu

These firms often have strong need for:

- high-volume SMS and notification reach
- outbound voice and service reminder flows
- compliance-aware contact strategy
- AI客服 / 质检 / 坐席辅助
- AI workflow orchestration and model routing

## High-fit signals

- 客服平台升级
- 智能质检
- 智能催收
- 外呼平台
- 通知 / 触达 / 运营平台
- knowledge base and SOP automation
- AI compliance, routing, audit, and controllable deployment

## Company patterns to look for

- 持牌消费金融公司

- 催收 / 法催 / 电催 / 回访服务商

## Output requirement

For these leads, always clarify:

- is the buyer licensed financial institution or service provider
- is the demand collections-heavy, customer-service-heavy, or growth-heavy
- is Baiwu’s lead product SMS / voice / AI agent / LLM routing
- is there likely need for local implementation or partner support
